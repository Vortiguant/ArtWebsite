Meet Sunita

Sunita Kamal is a Mumbai-born artist whose studio marries plein-air sketches, botanical research, and textile sampling. The result is a portfolio of warm, optimistic work that collectors describe as “portable sunshine.” Her Instagram journal, Art and sunny things, documents every glaze test, swatch, and commission milestone.

Whether she is hand-lettering holiday cards, carving koi-inspired trinket trays, or building layered landscape paintings, Sunita favors sustainable pigments, cotton rag paper, and clay bodies sourced from Rajasthani cooperatives. She collaborates with stylists, hospitality brands, and design-minded families who want art that feels handcrafted yet contemporary.
